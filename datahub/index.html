Surrey DataHub

Leading the Digital Innovation Hub for Animal Health (DIHAH), I am the Director of Surrey DataHub.

We work on data analytics and AI applications that enhance the interpretation of diverse animal and veterinary data. This encompasses information from veterinary records, wearables, and vast social media analyses.
